---Standardized Romanian Transcription Rules-------------------------------------

FINAL PALATAL CONSONANTSː should be transcribed with a separate segmental jod [j]

VERB FORMSː should not include final -[r e] or initial [a]- 

DIPHTONGSː 
	e a --> e̯ 'a		e a u --> e̯ 'a w		V i --> 'V j 		
	o a --> o̯ 'a		i a u --> j 'a w		V u --> 'V w

USE OF Gː g automatically becomes ɡ (Keyman: g<)

GLIDES
u̯ --> w
